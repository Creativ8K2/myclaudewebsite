About Kim

A proud owner of a resting thinking face.

Kim Alsup is an introvert stand-up comedian and comedy writer based in LA. She was born and raised in Detroit, MI which means she came pre-installed with paranoia and trust issues.

As a comedy writer, Kim has a knack for finding the funny in everyday life. From working at a temp agency in Atlanta, GA to ending up in a cult and not realizing it, she has stories to tell forever.
